Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Our Privacy Policy sets out how we use and protect any information that you give us when you use this website. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Unless you directly give us consent to do otherwise, we will only collect and use your personal information as set out below.

Our privacy policy is intended to comply fully with the Privacy Act 1988.

Collecting Personal Information

The type of information we collect from you depends on how you use our website. Personal Information collected that can be used to identify you as an individual will not be shared outside of Auto-Man Driving School.

Online Bookings

When you create an account with us to book your lesson, we need to collect personal information from you including name, address, phone number and email address.

We may contact you at the email address which you provide to us in order to:

  • • Provide you with updated information about our website; or
  • • Provide you with other information about our services which you may enjoy.

If you are receiving promotional information and do not wish to receive this information any longer, you may remove your name either by clicking the unsubscribe button at the bottom of a promotional email or e-mailing us.

Please allow 5 business days for this request to be processed.

Cookies

Cookies are used on this website to collect information on how you use the site. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies. When you visit automandrivingschool.com.au our servers along with Google Analytics and other third-party programs track and record information that your browser sends. This data may include: your IP address, pages viewed, product viewed, referring sites, time spent on site, product purchase information and other statistics relating to browsing behaviours.

Cookies are used to provide functionality and to recognise you across different services and devices. They enable us to identify you and personalize and enhance your experience.

If you do not wish to receive any cookies you may set your browser to refuse cookies. Please note; if you do not accept cookies, you may have difficulty using some site functions.

Facebook Pixel

Conversion tracking helps us measure the return on investment of Facebook Ads by reporting on the actions people take after viewing those ads. We create pixels that track conversions, add them to the pages of our website where the conversions happen, and then track these conversions back to ads we are running on Facebook.

No personal information is contained in or collected as a result of using these cookies or pixels.

To opt-out from Facebook's interest-based ads follow these instructions from Facebook.

Aggregation of Data

We may collect data, including personal information from our customers. This may be used to generate reports, statistics, and other data, to which we retain all intellectual property rights. You grant to us a royalty-free, worldwide, non-exclusive licence to aggregate that data with other data, including from other customers, as long as your information remains anonymous and you cannot be identified.

Please note the section on Cookies above which details the limited circumstances in which your information may be aggregated and used in connection with your identity. In all use of data, we comply with, and your rights are protected by, the Privacy Act 1988.

Payment Information

When you book a lesson with us online you will provide information such as your name and contact details. We use this information to identify and process your booking. When a booking is made through our website, customer credit card details are collected and stored by our payment provider which facilitates further use of your credit card at checkout.

Only the first and last four digits of the card can be viewed by Auto-Man Driving School staff to assist in the identification of the customer.

Secure Online Payment

There are a number of payment methods you can use when making a booking on our website. If you are directed to a third-party website, their privacy policy will govern the use of your information.

The payment process through our website is protected by industry-standard encryption. The payment process is protected by industry-standard encryption between the customers' browser and our website, and again between our website and the payment gateway. This prevents transaction information from being intercepted as it travels between the respective servers/computers.

Access to Information

If at any time you want to know exactly what personal information we hold about you, you are welcome to access this by e-mailing us.

This information will be made available to you within 5 business days.

If at any time you wish to change personal information, please contact us by e-mailing us and we will amend this record. If you wish to have your personal information deleted, please email us and we will take all reasonable steps to delete it unless we need to keep it for legal reasons.
