Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Our Privacy Policy sets out how we use and protect any information that you give us when you use this website. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Unless you directly give us consent to do otherwise, we will only collect and use your personal information as set out below.

Our privacy policy is intended to comply fully with the Privacy Act 1988.

Collecting Personal Information

The type of information we collect from you depends on how you use our website. Personal Information collected that can be used to identify you as an individual will not be shared outside of Auto-Man Driving School.

Online Bookings

When you make a booking with us, we collect essential information such as your name, email address, mobile number and pickup location. This information is required so we can manage your booking and contact you about your lesson.

Before your first lesson you will need to complete an intake form. This form collects information that instructors must have before conducting a driving lesson, including your licence or learner permit number, any medical conditions that may affect your driving and details about your driving experience or preferences.

Your information is used only for managing your lessons and providing a safe and suitable learning experience.

Cookies

Cookies are used on this website to collect information on how you use the site. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies. When you visit automandrivingschool.com.au our servers along with Google Analytics and other third-party programs track and record information that your browser sends. This data may include: your IP address, pages viewed, product viewed, referring sites, time spent on site, product purchase information and other statistics relating to browsing behaviours.

Cookies are used to provide functionality and to recognise you across different services and devices. They enable us to identify you and personalize and enhance your experience.

If you do not wish to receive any cookies you may set your browser to refuse cookies. Please note; if you do not accept cookies, you may have difficulty using some site functions.

Payment Information

When you book a lesson, any payment is processed securely through Google's booking system using Stripe. Auto-Man Driving School does not collect, store or have access to your credit card details at any time.

All payment information is handled directly by Stripe, a certified PCI-compliant payment provider. Stripe processes and secures the transaction. Auto-Man Driving School does not receive any payment details, transaction data, or notifications from Stripe and cannot access your card number or any other sensitive information.

Access to Information

If at any time you want to know what personal information we hold about you, please contact us by email.

This information will be made available to you within 5 business days.

Changing and Deleting Information We Hold

If you need to update any personal information, please email us and we will make the necessary changes.

If you wish to have your personal information deleted, please email us and we will take all reasonable steps to delete it unless we are required to keep it for legal reasons.

Storage of Your Information

We take reasonable steps to keep your personal information secure and up to date. All information is stored on secure cloud servers provided by Supabase, which maintains industry-standard security protections.

Access to your information is limited to authorised Auto-Man Driving School staff who require it to manage your bookings and lessons.

Password and Login

When you create an account on our website you use your email address and choose your own password. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and ensuring that your login details are not shared with anyone. Auto-Man Driving School cannot access your password and cannot recover it for you.

Links to External Websites

Our website may have links to external websites. These linked sites are not under our control, and we are not responsible for the conduct of companies linked to our website. Before disclosing your personal information on any other website, we advise you to read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service of those websites.

Changes to This Policy

From time to time, our policies will be reviewed and may be revised. We reserve the right to change our Privacy Policy at any time and notify you by posting an updated version of the policy on our website.

By using our site, you consent to our Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Policy.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any queries about any of our policies, please don't hesitate to contact us.
