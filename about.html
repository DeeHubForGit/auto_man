Our Story

Auto-Man Driving School has been helping learners in Geelong and surrounding suburbs gain their driving independence with confidence. Our mission is to provide high-quality, patient, and personalised driving instruction that prepares students not just to pass their test, but to become safe, confident drivers for life.

We understand that learning to drive can be both exciting and nerve-wracking. That's why we focus on creating a supportive, pressure-free environment where students can learn at their own pace.
