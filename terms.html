Booking Terms & Conditions

Please read our terms carefully before booking your lesson

Cancellation Policy

To cancel or reschedule your lesson you must log into this website and change/cancel your booking.

Lessons cancelled or rescheduled within 24 hours of the scheduled start time will be charged the full fee (no refund).

You will also be charged the full fee if you:

  • • Are more than 15 minutes late for your lesson.
  • • Do not show up for your lesson (no-show).
  • • Are unfit to drive at the time of your lesson.
  • • Cannot provide a current Learners Permit at the time of your lesson.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be issued where a lesson is cancelled more than 24 hours prior.

Cancellations after this time will not be refunded.

Lesson Start Time

You are responsible for being on time for the commencement of your lesson.

If you are late your lesson will be shortened by the number of minutes you are late and will still end at the originally designated time.

Pricing

All prices include Goods & Services Tax (GST) at the current rate of 10%.

Unused Lessons

Prepaid lessons cannot be refunded, but they can be transferred to another person with written permission from you and Auto-Man Driving School.

Lesson Packages

Prepaid lesson packages expire after 12 months and can be used to book lessons any time.

Cancellation by Auto-Man Driving School

If Auto-Man Driving School needs to cancel or end your lesson early due to unforeseen circumstances, we will do one of the following:

  • • Provide you with a replacement lesson.
  • • Add the equivalent time lost to your next lesson.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our terms and conditions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
