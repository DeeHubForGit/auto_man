Q: How do I book my first lesson?
You can book online through our booking system or call us directly at {{PHONE}}. We'll discuss your experience level, availability, and arrange a convenient pick-up location.
Q: What do I need to bring to my first lesson?
You'll need your learner permit and a positive attitude! We recommend wearing comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. If you wear glasses or contacts, make sure to bring them.
Q: I'm a complete beginner. Is that okay?
Absolutely! We specialise in teaching complete beginners. We'll start with the basics in a quiet area and progress at your pace. Everyone starts somewhere, and we're here to guide you every step of the way.
Q: Where do lessons take place?
We service Geelong and surrounding suburbs. We'll pick you up from your preferred location (home, school, work) and conduct lessons on a variety of roads to build your confidence in different driving conditions.

Q: How much do lessons cost?
Individual lessons start at $85 for 1 hour, $125 for 1.5 hours, and $165 for 2 hours. We also offer package deals that provide better value. Check our Lesson Packages page for current pricing.
Q: How long should my lessons be?
We recommend 1.5 to 2-hour lessons for best results. This gives you enough time to warm up, practice skills, and consolidate learning without getting too tired. However, 1-hour lessons are also available if that suits your schedule better.
Q: Do you offer package deals?
Yes! We offer multi-lesson packages that provide better value. Popular options include 3-lesson, 5-lesson, and 10-lesson packages. These are valid for 12 months and can save you money compared to individual lessons.
Q: What's your cancellation policy?
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to change your lesson.

üìù Driving Test

Q: How many lessons do I need before taking my test?
This varies for each student depending on your starting level, natural ability, and practice between lessons. On average, students need 10-20 professional lessons plus regular practice with a supervising driver. We'll let you know when we think you're ready.
Q: Do you offer a driving test package?
Yes! Our Driving Test Package ($300) includes a pre-test warm-up lesson, use of our vehicle for the test, and we'll accompany you to VicRoads. This helps you feel comfortable and confident on test day. Learn more on our Driving Test Package page.
Q: Can I use your car for my driving test?
Yes, when you book our Driving Test Package, you'll use our vehicle for your test. This is beneficial because you'll be familiar with the car from your lessons, and our vehicles meet all VicRoads requirements.
Q: What if I fail my test?
Don't worry - many people don't pass on their first attempt! We'll review what went wrong, work on those areas, and help you prepare for your next test. The experience of taking the test is valuable learning in itself.
